# Quiz: Chemical Coordination

## Question 1
**Compare the nervous system and hormonal system.** (6 marks)

## Answer 1
- Nervous system uses electrical impulses; hormonal system uses chemical messengers
- Nervous system is fast (milliseconds); hormonal system is slow (seconds to hours)
- Nervous system effects are short-lived; hormonal system effects are long-lasting
- Nervous system uses neurones; hormonal system uses blood transport

---

## Question 2
**Explain how blood glucose concentration is controlled.** (8 marks)

## Answer 2
- Blood glucose monitored by pancreas
- **High glucose**: Pancreas secretes insulin → liver converts glucose to glycogen → blood glucose decreases
- **Low glucose**: Pancreas secretes glucagon → liver converts glycogen to glucose → blood glucose increases
- This is negative feedback (maintains constant level)

---

## Question 3
**Explain what happens when a person has Type 1 diabetes.** (4 marks)

## Answer 3
- Body cannot produce insulin
- Beta cells in pancreas destroyed
- Blood glucose remains high after eating
- Glucose appears in urine
- Treatment: Insulin injections needed
