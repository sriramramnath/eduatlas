# Chapter 7: Chemical Coordination in Animals

## Quick Summary

- **Hormones** are chemical messengers (slower than nerves but longer-lasting)
- **Pancreas** produces **insulin** (lowers glucose) and **glucagon** (raises glucose)
- **Negative feedback** maintains blood glucose levels
- **Type 1 diabetes**: Cannot produce insulin; **Type 2**: Cells don't respond to insulin
- **Adrenaline**: Fight or flight - increases heart rate, breathing, blood glucose
- **ADH**: Controls water balance by affecting kidney
