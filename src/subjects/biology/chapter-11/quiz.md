# Quiz: Plants and Food

## Question 1
**Describe how you would test a leaf for starch.** (6 marks)

## Answer 1
- Kill leaf in boiling water for 30 seconds - stops all reactions
- Turn off Bunsen burner - ethanol is flammable
- Place leaf in boiling ethanol - removes chlorophyll/green color
- Remove when pale/colorless
- Wash in cold water to soften
- Spread on tile, add iodine solution
- Blue-black color indicates starch present

## Question 2
**Explain how a leaf is adapted for photosynthesis.** (6 marks)

## Answer 2
- Large surface area to capture light
- Thin leaves for short diffusion distance
- Palisade mesophyll cells contain many chloroplasts
- Palisade cells near upper surface for maximum light
- Spongy mesophyll has air spaces for gas diffusion
- Stomata allow CO₂ in and O₂ out
- Veins bring water and remove glucose

## Question 3
**Explain why plants need nitrates.** (3 marks)

## Answer 3
- To make proteins
- To make DNA
- To make chlorophyll
- Deficiency causes stunted growth and yellow leaves
