# Chapter 11: Plants and Food

## Quick Summary

- **Photosynthesis**: CO₂ + H₂O → Glucose + O₂ (light + chlorophyll)
- **Limiting factors**: Light, CO₂, Temperature
- **Leaf adaptations**: Chloroplasts, stomata, thin for gas exchange
- **Starch test**: Boil in ethanol to remove chlorophyll → add iodine → blue-black = starch
- **Glucose uses**: Respiration, starch storage, cellulose, proteins
- **Mineral ions**: Nitrates (proteins), Magnesium (chlorophyll)
