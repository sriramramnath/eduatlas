# Chapter 10: Transport in Plants

## Quick Summary

- **Xylem**: Dead cells, transports water + minerals UPWARDS only
- **Phloem**: Living cells, transports sugars + amino acids BIDIRECTIONALLY
- **Transpiration**: Water loss from leaves → creates pull for water movement
- **Cohesion-tension**: Water molecules stick together, pulled up xylem
- **Stomata**: Pores controlled by guard cells, open in light, close in dark/drought
- **Root hairs**: Increase surface area for water absorption
