- **Heart**: 4 chambers (2 atria, 2 ventricles), valves prevent backflow
- **Arteries**: thick walls, carry blood away from heart (high pressure)
- **Veins**: thin walls, carry blood to heart, have valves
- **Capillaries**: one cell thick, site of exchange
- **Red blood cells**: biconcave, no nucleus, carry haemoglobin
- **White blood cells**: phagocytes (engulf) and lymphocytes (antibodies)
