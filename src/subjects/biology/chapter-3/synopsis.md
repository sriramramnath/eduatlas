# Chapter 3: Breathing and Gas Exchange

## Quick Summary

- The gas exchange system includes: trachea, bronchi, bronchioles, and alveoli
- **Inhalation**: External intercostals contract + diaphragm flattens → volume increases → pressure decreases → air enters
- **Exhalation**: Muscles relax → volume decreases → pressure increases → air exits
- Alveoli are adapted for gas exchange with large surface area, thin walls, and rich blood supply
- **Smoking causes**: bronchitis, emphysema, lung cancer, heart disease
- **Breathing ≠ Respiration** (mechanical vs chemical)
