# Chapter 6: Coordination

## Quick Summary

- **Stimulus → Receptor → CNS → Effector → Response**
- **Reflex actions** are automatic, fast, and don't involve the brain (spinal cord only)
- **Synapses** use neurotransmitters to pass impulses between neurones (one-way)
- **Eye**: Cornea refracts light, iris controls pupil, lens focuses on retina
- **Near vision**: Ciliary muscles contract → lens fat/thick
- **Distant vision**: Ciliary muscles relax → lens thin/flat
