# Quiz: Coordination

## Question 1
**Describe a reflex action, using the example of withdrawing a hand from a hot object.** (5 marks)

## Answer 1
- Heat detected by receptors in skin
- Impulse travels along sensory neurone to spinal cord
- Impulse passes through relay neurone (synapse)
- Impulse travels along motor neurone to arm muscles
- Muscles contract, hand moves away
- Automatic response, doesn't involve brain

---

## Question 2
**Explain how the eye focuses on near and distant objects.** (4 marks)

## Answer 2
- **Near objects**: Ciliary muscles contract, suspensory ligaments slacken, lens becomes fatter/more curved, light rays converge more
- **Distant objects**: Ciliary muscles relax, suspensory ligaments tighten, lens becomes thinner/less curved, light rays converge less

---

## Question 3
**Explain the role of synapses in the nervous system.** (3 marks)

## Answer 3
- Gap between neurones
- Neurotransmitters allow impulses to cross synapse
- Ensures one-way transmission of impulses
- Allows integration of signals from multiple neurones
