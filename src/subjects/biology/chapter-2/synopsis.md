# Chapter 2: The Variety of Living Organisms

## Quick Summary

- Living organisms are classified into **five kingdoms**: Animals, Plants, Fungi, Protoctists, and Bacteria
- **Eukaryotes** have a nucleus; **Prokaryotes** do not
- **Viruses are not considered living** - they cannot carry out life processes independently
- **Bacteria** are prokaryotic, single-celled organisms
- **Fungi** have cell walls made of **chitin** (not cellulose)
- Plants store **starch**, animals store **glycogen**
