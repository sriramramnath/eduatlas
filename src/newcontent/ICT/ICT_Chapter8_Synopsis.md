# IGCSE ICT — Chapter 8: Online Communities
## Concise Synopsis (Pearson Edexcel Specification)

---

## Quick Reference Summary

### 1. Types of Online Communities

| Type | Purpose | Examples |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Social Networks** | General connection and sharing | Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn |
| **Interest-Based** | Shared hobbies/passions | Reddit, Discord, gaming forums |
| **Support Groups** | Help and emotional support | Health forums, bereavement groups |
| **Professional** | Career development | LinkedIn, GitHub, Stack Overflow |
| **Educational** | Learning collaboration | MOOC forums, VLEs, study groups |

---

### 2. Social Networking Platforms

| Platform | Primary Use | Key Features |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| Facebook | General networking | Profiles, groups, events |
| Instagram | Photo/video sharing | Stories, reels, filters |
| Twitter/X | Microblogging | Short posts, hashtags |
| LinkedIn | Professional networking | CVs, job listings |
| TikTok | Short-form video | Creative content, trends |
| Snapchat | Ephemeral messaging | Disappearing messages |

---

### 3. Benefits of Online Communities

| Category | Benefits |
|----------|----------|
| **Social** | Connect globally, find like-minded people, inclusion |
| **Information** | Access expertise, collective knowledge, stay informed |
| **Personal Development** | Skill learning, creative expression, support |
| **Support** | Emotional help, practical advice, 24/7 availability |

---

### 4. Risks of Online Communities

| Category | Risks |
|----------|-------|
| **Safety** | Predators, identity theft, scams, grooming |
| **Social** | Cyberbullying, toxic communities, misinformation |
| **Privacy** | Data collection, unintended disclosure, permanent records |
| **Psychological** | Addiction, negative comparison, echo chambers |

---

### 5. Netiquette Principles

```
CORE NETIQUETTE RULES:
✓ Treat others with RESPECT
✓ Communicate CLEARLY
✓ Protect PRIVACY (yours and others')
✓ Stay on TOPIC
✓ Think before you POST
✓ Remember there's a real person behind the screen
```

---

### 6. Protecting Yourself Online

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Limit profile information** | Reduce exposure to strangers |
| **Use privacy settings** | Control who sees your content |
| **Verify identities** | Protect against fake accounts |
| **Think before posting** | Manage digital footprint |
| **Report concerns** | Alert moderators to problems |

---

### 7. Community Moderation

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Moderators** | Enforce rules, remove content, warn/ban users |
| **Community Rules** | Define acceptable behaviour |
| **Reporting Systems** | Allow users to flag problems |
| **Appeal Processes** | Fair review of moderation decisions |

---

### 8. Support Communities

| Type | Purpose | Examples |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Health** | Medical condition support | Cancer forums, mental health |
| **Addiction** | Recovery support | AA online, recovery groups |
| **Bereavement** | Grief support | Loss support forums |
| **Parenting** | Parent advice | Mumsnet, parenting forums |
| **Professional** | Career help | LinkedIn groups, industry forums |

---

### 9. High-Yield Exam Points

**Must-Know Facts:**

- **Online communities connect globally** — overcome geographical barriers
- **Benefits AND risks** — always consider both sides
- **Netiquette = online etiquette** — appropriate behaviour rules
- **Privacy protection essential** — limit personal information shared
- **Cyberbullying is a major risk** — report and seek help
- **Digital footprint is permanent** — think before posting
- **Moderators enforce rules** — maintain community standards

**Key Distinctions:**

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| What connects people professionally? | LinkedIn, GitHub |
| What helps with health issues? | Support communities |
| What is online etiquette called? | Netiquette |
| What protects community standards? | Moderators |

---

### 10. Quick Definition List

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Online Community** | Group interacting via internet around shared interests |
| **Social Network** | Platform for connections and content sharing |
| **Netiquette** | Online communication etiquette |
| **Cyberbullying** | Digital harassment |
| **Echo Chamber** | Environment reinforcing existing beliefs |
| **Moderator** | Person enforcing community rules |
| **Digital Footprint** | Permanent record of online activities |
| **Grooming** | Building trust for exploitation |

---

### 11. Exam Tips

1. **Balance benefits and risks** — don't be one-sided
2. **Use specific examples** — name platforms and community types
3. **Mention netiquette** — appropriate behaviour matters
4. **Consider vulnerable groups** — different impacts on different people
5. **Address privacy** — how to protect personal information
6. **Think about moderation** — how communities stay safe
