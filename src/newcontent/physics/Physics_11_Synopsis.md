# Chapter 11: The Electromagnetic Spectrum
## Pearson Edexcel IGCSE Physics - Concise Synopsis

---

## Common Properties of EM Waves

1. All are **transverse**
2. All travel at **3 × 10⁸ m/s** in vacuum
3. Can travel through **vacuum**
4. Transfer **energy**
5. Obey **v = fλ**
6. Can be **reflected, refracted, diffracted**

---

## The Electromagnetic Spectrum

### Order (Long λ → Short λ)

```
Radio → Microwaves → Infrared → Visible → UV → X-rays → Gamma
```

### Summary Table

| Type | λ (approx) | Key Uses | Hazards |
|------|------------|----------|---------|
| **Radio** | > 1 m | Communication, TV | None |
| **Microwaves** | 1 mm - 1 m | Mobiles, cooking, radar | Heating |
| **Infrared** | 700 nm - 1 mm | Heating, remote, thermal | Burns |
| **Visible** | 400-700 nm | Vision, photography | None |
| **UV** | 10-400 nm | Tanning, sterilization | Skin/eye damage |
| **X-rays** | 0.01-10 nm | Medical imaging | Cell damage |
| **Gamma** | < 0.01 nm | Cancer treatment | Severe damage |

---

## Visible Light Spectrum

```
Red (700 nm) → Orange → Yellow → Green → Blue → Indigo → Violet (400 nm)
```

- **Red**: Longest λ, lowest f in visible
- **Violet**: Shortest λ, highest f in visible

### Radio Waves
- Radio/TV broadcasting
- Mobile phone communication
- Radar
- Satellite communication

### Microwaves
- Microwave ovens (heat water)
- Mobile phones
- Satellite communication
- Radar

### Infrared
- Thermal imaging
- Remote controls
- Electric heaters
- Fibre optics
- Night vision

### Visible Light
- Human vision
- Photography
- Optical fibres
- Photosynthesis

### Ultraviolet
- Sun tanning
- Sterilization
- Security marking
- Fluorescent lamps

### X-rays
- Medical imaging (bones)
- Airport security
- Industrial inspection
- Crystallography

### Gamma Rays
- Cancer treatment (radiotherapy)
- Sterilization
- Food preservation
- Medical tracers

## Hazards and Protection

### Ionizing Radiation
**Types:** UV, X-rays, Gamma rays

**Hazards:**
- DNA damage
- Cell damage
- Cancer risk

**Protection:**
- Minimize exposure time
- Maximize distance
- Use shielding (lead, concrete)
- Wear protective clothing

### UV Protection
- Sunscreen
- Sunglasses
- Limit exposure

### X-ray Protection
- Lead aprons
- Minimize exposure
- Shielding
