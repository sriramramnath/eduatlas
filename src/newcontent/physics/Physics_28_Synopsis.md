## Doppler Effect and Redshift

### Moving Source
- **Toward observer:** Higher frequency (blueshift)
- **Away from observer:** Lower frequency (redshift)

### Redshift Formula
```
z = Δλ/λ
```

---

## Hubble's Law

```
v = H₀ × d
```
- v = recession velocity
- H₀ = Hubble constant (~70 km/s/Mpc)
- d = distance

**Implication:** Universe is expanding

---

## Big Bang Theory

### Main Points
- Universe began ~13.8 billion years ago
- Extremely hot, dense state
- Has been expanding ever since

### Evidence
| Evidence | What it shows |
|----------|---------------|
| Redshift | Universe expanding |
| CMB | Early universe radiation |
| Light elements | Formed in Big Bang |

### Cosmic Microwave Background
- Temperature: 2.7 K
- Formed 380,000 years after Big Bang
