# Chapter 19: Solids, Liquids and Gases
## Pearson Edexcel IGCSE Physics - Concise Synopsis

---

## States of Matter

| State | Shape | Volume | Particles |
|-------|-------|--------|-----------|
| **Solid** | Fixed | Fixed | Vibrate in fixed positions |
| **Liquid** | Container | Fixed | Move past each other |
| **Gas** | Container | Fills container | Move freely, far apart |

---

## Changes of State

| Process | Change | Energy | Temperature |
|---------|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Melting** | S → L | Absorbed | Constant |
| **Freezing** | L → S | Released | Constant |
| **Boiling** | L → G | Absorbed | Constant |
| **Condensation** | G → L | Released | Constant |
| **Evaporation** | L → G | Absorbed | Any temp |

### Key Point
Temperature **constant** during change of state.

---

## Gas Laws

### Boyle's Law (Constant Temperature)
```
p₁ × V₁ = p₂ × V₂
```

### Pressure-Temperature Law (Constant Volume)
```
p₁/T₁ = p₂/T₂
(T in Kelvin)
```

---

## Gas Pressure

### Cause
- Particle collisions with container walls

### Factors
| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Temperature ↑ | Pressure ↑ |
| Volume ↓ | Pressure ↑ |
| More particles | Pressure ↑ |

---

## Brownian Motion

- Smoke particles move randomly
- Evidence for kinetic theory
- Shows particles are in constant motion

---

## Evaporation vs Boiling

| Feature | Evaporation | Boiling |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Temperature | Any | Boiling point |
| Location | Surface only | Throughout |
| Bubbles | No | Yes |

### Cooling Effect
- Fastest particles escape
- Average KE decreases
- Temperature falls

---

## Temperature Scales

| | °C | K |
|--|----|---|
| Absolute zero | -273 | 0 |
| Freezing point | 0 | 273 |
| Boiling point | 100 | 373 |

### Conversion
```
K = °C + 273
°C = K - 273
```
