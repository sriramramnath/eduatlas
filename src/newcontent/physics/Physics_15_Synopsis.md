## Essential Equations

```
Specific heat capacity:   E = m × c × Δθ
Latent heat (fusion):     E = m × Lf
Latent heat (vaporization): E = m × Lv
Temperature conversion:   K = °C + 273
```

## Temperature and Heat

| Term | Definition | Unit |
|------|------------|------|
| **Temperature** | How hot/cold | °C or K |
| **Heat** | Energy transferred | J |
| **Thermal energy** | Total KE of particles | J |

### Temperature Scales

| | Celsius | Kelvin |
|--|---------|--------|
| Water freezes | 0°C | 273 K |
| Water boils | 100°C | 373 K |
| Absolute zero | -273°C | 0 K |

## Heat Transfer Methods

### Conduction
- Through material, no material movement
- Metals = good conductors
- Wood/plastic/air = insulators

### Convection
- Through fluid movement
- Hot fluid rises, cool fluid sinks
- Creates convection currents

### Radiation
- By infrared waves (EM)
- No medium needed
- Can travel through vacuum

### Surface Properties

| Surface | Emission | Absorption |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Dark, matte | Best | Best |
| Light, shiny | Poor | Poor |

---

## Specific Heat Capacity

### Definition
Energy to raise 1 kg by 1°C.

### Equation
```
E = m × c × Δθ
```

### Values

| Substance | c (J/kg°C) |
|-----------|------------|
| Water | 4200 |
| Aluminum | 900 |
| Iron | 450 |
| Copper | 390 |

### Example
```
Heat 3 kg water from 20°C to 60°C:
E = 3 × 4200 × 40 = 504,000 J
```

---

## Specific Latent Heat

### Changes of State
- Temperature **constant** during change
- Energy breaks/form bonds

### Fusion (melting)
```
E = m × Lf
```
Water: Lf = 334,000 J/kg

### Vaporization (boiling)
```
E = m × Lv
```
Water: Lv = 2,260,000 J/kg

### Example
```
Melt 2 kg ice:
E = 2 × 334,000 = 668,000 J
```

### Methods

| Method | How it Works |

| Draught excluders | Stops convection |

- Lower = better insulator
- Higher = poorer insulator

## Key Exam Points

1. **Three methods** - conduction, convection, radiation

3. **Latent heat** - temp constant during change
4. **K = °C + 273** - temperature conversion
5. **Surfaces** - dark/matt good, light/shiny poor
6. **Water SHC** - 4200 J/kg°C (very high)
