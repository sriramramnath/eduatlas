## Gravitational Force

### Newton's Law
```
F = G × m₁ × m₂ / r²
```

### Inverse Square Law
- Distance doubles → Force = ¼
- Distance triples → Force = ¹⁄₉

---

## Orbits

### Orbital Speed
```
v = √(GM/r)
```
- Smaller radius → Faster speed
- Larger radius → Slower speed

### Geostationary Satellites
- Altitude: 35,786 km
- Period: 24 hours
- Fixed position above equator

## The Moon

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|

### Eclipses
- **Solar:** Moon blocks Sun
- **Lunar:** Earth blocks Sun's light from Moon
