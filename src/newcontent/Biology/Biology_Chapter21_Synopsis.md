# Chapter 21: Genetic Modification (Biotechnology) - Concise Synopsis

## Quick Revision Guide for Pearson Edexcel IGCSE Biology

---

## 1. Definition

**Genetic Modification (Genetic Engineering)**
Altering an organism's DNA to introduce desirable characteristics.

---

## 2. Process of Genetic Engineering

```
1. Identify desired gene
         ↓
2. Cut gene using restriction enzymes
         ↓
3. Insert into vector (plasmid)
         ↓
4. Insert vector into host cell
         ↓
5. Host cell reproduces
         ↓
6. Organism expresses new gene
```

### Key Enzymes
| Enzyme | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **Restriction enzymes** | Cut DNA at specific points |
| **DNA ligase** | Join DNA pieces together |

---

## 3. Examples

### Medical
| Application | Benefit |
|-------------|---------|
| **GM bacteria produce insulin** | Human insulin for diabetics |
| **Gene therapy** | Treat genetic disorders |
| **Vaccines** | Prevent disease |

### Agricultural
| Crop | Modification | Benefit |
|------|--------------|---------|
| **Golden Rice** | Beta-carotene gene | Vitamin A |
| **Bt crops** | Bt gene | Pest resistance |
| **Herbicide-resistant** | Herbicide resistance | Weed control |

---

## 4. Advantages

- Higher yields
- Disease resistance
- Reduced pesticide use
- Medical products (insulin)
- Enhanced nutrition
- Crops in harsh conditions

---

## 5. Concerns

### Environmental
- Gene transfer to wild plants
- Impact on biodiversity
- "Super weeds"

### Health
- Potential allergies
- Unknown long-term effects

### Ethical
- Playing God
- Animal welfare
- Patenting life

---

## 6. Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Transgenic** | Organism with foreign DNA |
| **Vector** | Carrier for gene transfer |
| **Plasmid** | Circular bacterial DNA |
| **Gene therapy** | Treating genetic disorders with genes |

---

## 7. Exam Focus Points

### High-Yield Facts
1. Genetic engineering alters DNA directly
2. Vectors needed to transfer genes
3. Bacteria produce human insulin
4. GM crops: Golden Rice, Bt crops
5. Both advantages AND concerns important

### Common Mistakes
- Confusing with selective breeding
- Forgetting vectors
- Not naming enzymes
- Only discussing benefits
- Not giving specific examples
