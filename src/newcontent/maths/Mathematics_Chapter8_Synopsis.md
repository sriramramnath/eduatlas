## 📐 PART 1: UNIT CONVERSIONS

### Length Conversions

| Conversion | Operation |
|------------|-----------|
| km → m | × 1000 |
| m → cm | × 100 |
| cm → mm | × 10 |
| m → mm | × 1000 |
| km → cm | × 100000 |

**Quick Rule:** Larger → Smaller = MULTIPLY | Smaller → Larger = DIVIDE

---

### Area Conversions ⭐ SQUARE the linear factor

| Conversion | Factor |
|------------|--------|
| km² → m² | × 1,000,000 |
| m² → cm² | × 10,000 |
| cm² → mm² | × 100 |
| m² → mm² | × 1,000,000 |

**Formula:** Area factor = $(\text{linear factor})^2$

**Example:** $1 \text{ m} = 100 \text{ cm}$, so $1 \text{ m}^2 = 100^2 = 10000 \text{ cm}^2$

---

### Volume Conversions ⭐ CUBE the linear factor

| Conversion | Factor |
|------------|--------|
| km³ → m³ | × 1,000,000,000 |
| m³ → cm³ | × 1,000,000 |
| cm³ → mm³ | × 1,000 |

**Formula:** Volume factor = $(\text{linear factor})^3$

**Example:** $1 \text{ m} = 100 \text{ cm}$, so $1 \text{ m}^3 = 100^3 = 1000000 \text{ cm}^3$

---

### Capacity Conversions

| Conversion | Relationship |
|------------|--------------|
| cm³ ↔ ml | **1 cm³ = 1 ml** (same value!) |
| 1000 cm³ | = 1000 ml = **1 litre** |
| 1 m³ | = **1000 litres** |

## ⚡ PART 2: COMPOUND MEASURES

### The Three Key Formulas

| Measure | Formula | Rearranged |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **Speed** | $S = \frac{D}{T}$ | $D = S \times T$, $T = \frac{D}{S}$ |
| **Density** | $\rho = \frac{m}{V}$ | $m = \rho \times V$, $V = \frac{m}{\rho}$ |
| **Pressure** | $P = \frac{F}{A}$ | $F = P \times A$, $A = \frac{F}{P}$ |

---

### Speed Quick Reference

| Units | Use |
|-------|-----|
| m/s | Metres per second |
| km/h | Kilometres per hour |
| mph | Miles per hour |

**Speed Unit Conversion:**
- km/h → m/s: **÷ 3.6** (or × $\frac{5}{18}$)
- m/s → km/h: **× 3.6** (or × $\frac{18}{5}$)

**Average Speed:** $\frac{\text{Total Distance}}{\text{Total Time}}$ (NOT average of speeds!)

---

### Density Quick Reference

| Units | Use |
|-------|-----|
| g/cm³ | Grams per cubic centimetre |
| kg/m³ | Kilograms per cubic metre |

**Key Conversion:** $1 \text{ g/cm}^3 = 1000 \text{ kg/m}^3$

**Water density:** $1 \text{ g/cm}^3 = 1000 \text{ kg/m}^3$

---

### Pressure Quick Reference

| Units | Use |
|-------|-----|
| N/m² | Newtons per square metre = **Pascal (Pa)** |
| N/cm² | Newtons per square centimetre |

**Key Unit:** 1 Pa = 1 N/m²

**Important:** Convert area to matching units!

## 🔢 FORMULA TRIANGLE METHOD

```
     Top (to find)
    ┌──────────┐
    │    ÷     │
    │          │
────┼──────────┼────
Left│    ×     │Right
```

**How to use:**
- Cover what you need to find
- What remains gives the formula
- Top ÷ Bottom, or Left × Right

### For Speed: Top = Distance, Left = Speed, Right = Time
### For Density: Top = Mass, Left = Density, Right = Volume
### For Pressure: Top = Force, Left = Pressure, Right = Area

##  TOP 5 EXAM PITFALLS

1. **Area/Volume confusion**
   - Area: Multiply by $100^2 = 10000$ for m² → cm²
   - Volume: Multiply by $100^3 = 1000000$ for m³ → cm³

2. **Inconsistent units**
   - Always convert to same unit before calculating

3. **Average speed mistake**
   - Use Total Distance ÷ Total Time, not $(v_1 + v_2)/2$

4. **Speed unit conversions**
   - Remember: km/h → m/s means dividing by 3.6

5. **Pressure unit matching**
   - Pressure in N/m² needs area in m² (not cm²)

## 📝 QUICK MEMORY AIDS

### Area vs Volume Conversion Factors
- **Area = 2D** → Square the factor ($^2$)
- **Volume = 3D** → Cube the factor ($^3$)

### Compound Measure Triangles
- **S**peed **D**istance **T**ime → **SDT**
- **D**ensity **M**ass **V**olume → **DMV**
- **P**ressure **F**orce **A**rea → **PFA**

### Speed Conversion Shortcut
- "K" for km/h has more letters than "m" for m/s
- So the number is bigger: 36 km/h = 10 m/s
