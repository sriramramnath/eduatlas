## Part A: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

### Question 1
What is the relative charge of a proton?
A) -1
B) 0
C) +1
D) +2

### Question 2
**Answer: C**
**Explanation**: Electron
- Electrons have a relative mass of 1/1836, which is considered negligible compared to protons and neutrons (both mass = 1).Which subatomic particle has negligible mass?
A) Proton
B) Neutron
C) Electron
D) Nucleus

**Answer: C**

**Explanation**: Electron

---

### Question 3
**Answer: B**
**Explanation**: Number of protons
- The atomic number (Z) represents the number of protons in the nucleus. This determines the element's identity.What does the atomic number represent?
A) Number of neutrons
B) Number of protons
C) Number of electrons and neutrons
D) Mass of the atom

**Answer: B**

**Explanation**: Number of protons

---

### Question 4
**Answer: C**
**Explanation**: 35
- Mass number = protons + neutrons = 17 + 18 = 35An atom has 17 protons, 18 neutrons and 17 electrons. What is its mass number?
A) 17
B) 34
C) 35
D) 52

**Answer: C**

**Explanation**: 35

### Question 5
**Answer: C**
**Explanation**: They have the same number of protons but different neutrons
- Isotopes are atoms of the same element (same atomic number) with different mass numbers (different neutrons). They have identical chemical properties.Which statement about isotopes is correct?
A) They have different numbers of protons
B) They have different chemical properties
C) They have the same number of protons but different neutrons
D) They have the same mass number

**Answer: C**

**Explanation**: They have the same number of protons but different neutrons

### Question 6
**Answer: A**
**Explanation**: 2,8,5
- Atomic number 15 = phosphorus. Following the 2,8,8 rule: 15 = 2 + 8 + 5, so configuration is 2,8,5.What is the electron configuration of an atom with atomic number 15?
A) 2,8,5
B) 2,8,4
C) 2,8,6
D) 2,7,6

**Answer: A**

**Explanation**: 2,8,5

---

### Question 7
**Answer: D**
**Explanation**: Proton and neutron
- The nucleus contains both protons and neutrons. Electrons orbit outside the nucleus in shells.Which particle is found in the nucleus of an atom?
A) Electron only
B) Proton only
C) Neutron only
D) Proton and neutron

**Answer: D**

**Explanation**: Proton and neutron

### Question 8
**Answer: A**
**Explanation**: 20
- Neutrons = Mass number - Atomic number = 40 - 20 = 20An element has the notation $_{20}^{40}Ca$. How many neutrons does it contain?

**Explanation**: 20

### Question 9
**Answer: B**

A) 2,8,9
B) 2,8,8,1
C) 2,9,8
D) 2,8,7,2

**Answer: B**

**Explanation**: 2,8,8,1
