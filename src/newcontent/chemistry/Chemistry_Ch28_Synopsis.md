## Why Are Esters Volatile?

1. **No hydrogen bonding** between ester molecules
2. **Only weak intermolecular forces** (dipole-dipole, van der Waals)
3. **Less energy needed** to evaporate
4. **Molecules escape easily** into air в†’ reach our nose в†’ detect odor
