##  Common Exam Traps

1. **Hydrocarbons = ONLY C and H** (no oxygen!)
2. **Saturated = single bonds only**; **Unsaturated = has C=C**
3. **Small molecules = LOW BP**, condense at TOP
4. **Bromine water test only works for alkenes**
5. **Prefixes**: Meth-1, Eth-2, Prop-3, But-4, Pent-5, Hex-6
6. **Alcohols end in -ol**; **Carboxylic acids end in -oic acid**
