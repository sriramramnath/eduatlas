### Question 2
**Answer: D**
**Explanation**: Al₂O₃
- Aluminium forms Al³⁺ ions, oxygen forms O²⁻ ions. Crossing charges: Al₂O₃ (charges balance: 2×+3 = 3×-2).What is the formula of aluminium oxide?
A) AlO
B) AlO₂
C) Al₂O
D) Al₂O₃

**Answer: D**

**Explanation**: Al₂O₃
